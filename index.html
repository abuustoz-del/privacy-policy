Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

Abu-Ustoz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • App Preferences: Language settings, theme preferences, and learning progress stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Google Play. We only receive confirmation of purchase status.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers for advertising

Data Storage

All app data (progress, settings, saved circuits) is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as an educational tool for learning electrical concepts.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device lock features to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
